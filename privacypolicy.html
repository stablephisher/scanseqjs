Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

About ScanSeq

ScanSeq is an open-source camera-based music sequencer created by stablephisher. It runs entirely in your browser.

Camera Data

ScanSeq accesses your webcam to capture video frames for real-time visual analysis. All camera processing happens locally in your browser. No camera data, face information, biometric data, or any video frames are ever transmitted, uploaded, or stored on any server. Your camera feed never leaves your device.

Cookies

ScanSeq may use cookies for basic functionality such as remembering your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

What cookies are

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.

Your choices regarding cookies

If you'd like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser:

  • Chrome: support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050
  • Firefox: support.mozilla.org
  • Safari: support.apple.com/kb/PH21411
  • Edge: Visit your browser's official help pages.

Data Collection

ScanSeq does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking scripts, and no user accounts.

Open Source

The full source code is available at github.com/stablephisher/scanseqjs. You can verify all data handling yourself.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, reach out via GitHub: @stablephisher
